Privacy Policy for Odoo Kaching

Odoo Kaching is a simple Chrome extension that plays a short “ka-ching” sound when an Odoo payment-success element appears on a page.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. It does not read the user's browsing history, form entries, identity, files, or any other personal information.

No Third-Party Sharing

The extension does not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties, because it does not collect any data at all.

Local-Only Operation

All functionality runs entirely within the user’s browser. The extension only listens for the presence of a specific DOM element (o_payment_successful) in order to play a local audio file. No information is sent over the network.

Permissions

Any permissions requested by the extension are solely used to detect the payment-success element on Odoo pages. They are not used to access or store user information.
